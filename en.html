2. Data Collection

This application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations and operations are performed locally on your device. No personal or sensitive user data is sent to external servers or third parties by this application.

3. Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (such as theme and unit settings) locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data is strictly functional, remains on your device, and is never uploaded to any server.

The application allows users to generate and download documents, such as the user manual and summaries of the data entered. These files are created and stored locally on the user's device. The application does not automatically transmit these files to external servers. Users are solely responsible for the storage, management, and any further sharing of downloaded files.

4. Data Security

Although this application does not collect personal data, all data stored locally on your device (preferences, generated files) is protected by Android's built-in security mechanisms and sandboxing, and is accessible only to this application.

5. Third-Party Services

This application does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. No data is shared with external providers by the application itself.

The application allows users to share data entered into the app, including generated summaries, through third-party applications installed on the device (such as WhatsApp). Data sharing is performed solely at the user's initiative. Once shared, the data is processed in accordance with the privacy policies of the selected third-party application. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of such services before sharing their data.

6. Cookies

This application does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.

7. User Rights (GDPR – EU Users)

As this application does not collect, process, or transmit any personal data to external servers, there is no personal data held by the developer that users would need to access, correct, or delete.

Users in the European Union are entitled to the rights provided under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectification, erasure, and portability of their personal data. Since no personal data is collected or stored by us, these rights apply only to any data you voluntarily share via third-party services (see Section 5).

For any privacy-related request or concern, you may contact us at: green.pepper.lab@gmail.com

8. Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. Since the application does not collect any personal data from any user, no special measures regarding children's data are required beyond this statement.
